About Us:

Corvus Entertainment is a unique company based on a utopian artist collective.Our purpose is to continually work toward each others successes as artists, with artists, for artists. Encouraging art is all its forms, Corvus encompasses music, visual art, film & performance. With a variety in membership, each component unique in style and influence, we come together as a whole unit to provide the world with an alternative to the norm. Corvus is a platform for self expression and personal truths, allowing creative freedom and individuality.
