Welcome our three newest members

12/28/15

Please join us in welcoming our three newest members: Shane, ArSkip & Shayne:


Though his heart is in his hometown of Ann Arbor, MI, rapper/singer Shane MauX (Belikos, Duk Duk Goose!) has called Brooklyn his home for the better part of a decade. Our team at Corvus Entertainment is elated to have Shane MauX as our newest member. played dozens of shows in the NYC Hip Hop scene and released two mix tapes and several videos. Click here to hear Shane's work.






From the beautiful island of St. Croix, ArSkip started freestyling for fun until a close friend suggested that he pursue music more seriously. He started writing songs, and dropped his first mixtape - "A Zillion Bars of Pain", hosted b G-Unit DJ Rob E Rob - in 2008. His new single "Why I Came Here" is tearing up clubs in the tri-state area, as well as satellite radio. Click here to hear ArSkip's work.



Shayne B. Bovell's undying love for art - from music to painting to set design - has made him a creative staple and one of the most influential figures in the Brooklyn artist community. Our members at Corvus Entertainment are elated to have him on our team. Click here to see Shayne's diverse artistic talents.
