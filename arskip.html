ArSkip


From the beautiful island of St. Croix, ArSkip started freestyling for fun until a close friend suggested that he pursue music more seriously. He started writing songs, and dropped his first mixtape - "A Zillion Bars of Pain", hosted b G-Unit DJ Rob E Rob - in 2008. His new single "Why I Came Here" is tearing up clubs in the tri-state area, as well as satellite radio. In 2016, ArSkip will release a series of mixtapes called "Short Drive on a Long Road". He will also be dropping his highly anticipated solo album, which is as yet untitled. His influences include Tupac, Biggie Smalls, A Tribe Called Quest, Bob Marley, Shabba Ranks, Buju Bonton, and Rakim.
