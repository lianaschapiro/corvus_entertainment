Shane MauX


Though his heart is in his hometown of Ann Arbor, MI, rapper/singer Shane MauX (Belikos, Duk Duk Goose!) has called Brooklyn his home for the better part of a decade. Shane's first solo release, the "Come Alive" EP is a departure from his previous projects. As the frontman of rock band Belikos, he played gigs including the Virgin Mobile Free Fest, CMJ, Webster Hall, Brooklyn Night Bazaar, Piano's, The Knitting Factory, Santos Party House, The Bitter End, and the Afro-Punk Festival. He shared bills with legendary artists including Ludacris, Mos Def, Bad Brains, Chromeo, M.I.A., Pavement, LCD Soundsystem, Joan Jett, and Fishbone. As one half of rap duo "Duk Duk Goose!", he played dozens of shows in the NYC Hip Hop scene and released two mix tapes and several videos.
