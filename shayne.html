Shayne B. Bovell



Shayne B. Bovell was born in 1972 in Georgetown, Guyana to parents Joan and James. Along with older sister Sabrina, he migrated to Brooklyn, NY. Shayne grew up in the East Flatbush section of Brooklyn. "I cant even remember but for all i know, i always have been drawing. Dont know what age - it was always there. That and my other friend that came along later. Four wheels and a deck."
